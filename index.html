About Us

If you lost your home during these days or you are a homeless, feel free to contact with Us. We'll be really glad if we can help any one of you. Contact us through you queries and grievances on this platform and we'll deliver your message to the nearby volunteer you need. We are ForThePeople, one of the first Indian Voluntary Organisation that works for the rural people whom are homeless. We are funded by many MNC companies and also from many individual donors. You can share your thoughts and suggestions with us about our mission and also support us finally by giving Us aid via PhonePe/GPay/Bank Transfer/NEFT.
